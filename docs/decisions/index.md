## Overview

Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) document significant architectural decisions made during the development of Ordinis. Each ADR captures the context, decision, and consequences of an architectural choice.

## ADR Template

When creating new ADRs, follow this structure:

```markdown
# ADR-XXX: Title

## Status
[Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded]

## Context
What is the issue or question that prompted this decision?

## Decision
What is the change that was decided upon?

## Consequences
What becomes easier or more difficult as a result of this decision?

## References
- Related documents or external resources
```
