# Financial Metrics Reference

## Performance Metrics

### Revenue Metrics
- **Revenue Growth Rate (YoY)**: (Current Period Revenue - Prior Period Revenue) / Prior Period Revenue × 100
- **CAGR (Compound Annual Growth Rate)**: ((Ending Value / Beginning Value)^(1/Number of Years) - 1) × 100
- **Revenue per Employee**: Total Revenue / Number of Employees
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Sales & Marketing Expenses / Number of New Customers
- **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)**: Average Revenue per Customer × Customer Lifespan × Gross Margin

### Profitability Metrics
- **Gross Margin**: (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue × 100
- **EBITDA Margin**: EBITDA / Revenue × 100
- **Operating Margin**: Operating Income / Revenue × 100
- **Net Profit Margin**: Net Income / Revenue × 100
- **Return on Assets (ROA)**: Net Income / Total Assets × 100
- **Return on Equity (ROE)**: Net Income / Shareholders' Equity × 100
- **Return on Invested Capital (ROIC)**: NOPAT / Invested Capital × 100

### Efficiency Metrics
- **Asset Turnover**: Revenue / Average Total Assets
- **Inventory Turnover**: COGS / Average Inventory
- **Days Sales Outstanding (DSO)**: (Accounts Receivable / Revenue) × 365
- **Cash Conversion Cycle**: DSO + Days Inventory Outstanding - Days Payable Outstanding
- **Working Capital Ratio**: Current Assets / Current Liabilities

## Valuation Metrics

### Earnings-Based Multiples
- **P/E Ratio (Price-to-Earnings)**: Market Cap / Net Income or Share Price / EPS
- **Forward P/E**: Current Share Price / Next Year Projected EPS
- **PEG Ratio**: P/E Ratio / Earnings Growth Rate
- **EV/EBITDA**: Enterprise Value / EBITDA

### Revenue-Based Multiples
- **P/S Ratio (Price-to-Sales)**: Market Cap / Total Revenue
- **EV/Revenue**: Enterprise Value / Total Revenue

### Asset-Based Multiples
- **P/B Ratio (Price-to-Book)**: Market Cap / Book Value of Equity
- **EV/IC (Enterprise Value to Invested Capital)**: EV / (Total Debt + Total Equity)

### Cash Flow Multiples
- **P/CF (Price-to-Cash Flow)**: Market Cap / Operating Cash Flow
- **EV/FCF (Enterprise Value to Free Cash Flow)**: EV / Free Cash Flow
- **FCF Yield**: Free Cash Flow / Market Cap × 100

## Growth Metrics

### Revenue & Profitability Growth
- **Revenue Growth Rate**: Period-over-period percentage change
- **EBITDA Growth Rate**: Period-over-period EBITDA change
- **EPS Growth Rate**: Period-over-period EPS change

### Operational Scaling
- **Rule of 40**: Revenue Growth Rate + EBITDA Margin (target ≥ 40 for SaaS)
- **Magic Number**: Net New ARR / Sales & Marketing Spend
- **Burn Multiple**: Net Burn / Net New ARR

## Enterprise Value Calculation

**Enterprise Value (EV)** = Market Cap + Total Debt + Minority Interest + Preferred Equity - Cash & Cash Equivalents

This represents the theoretical takeover price of a company.

## Industry-Specific Metrics

### Technology / SaaS
- **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)**: Sum of subscription revenue normalized annually
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**: Recurring subscription revenue per month
- **Net Revenue Retention (NRR)**: (Starting ARR + Expansion - Contraction - Churn) / Starting ARR × 100
- **Gross Revenue Retention (GRR)**: (Starting ARR - Churn) / Starting ARR × 100
- **LTV/CAC Ratio**: Customer Lifetime Value / Customer Acquisition Cost (target ≥ 3)
- **CAC Payback Period**: CAC / (ARPU × Gross Margin) in months

### E-Commerce / Retail
- **Same-Store Sales Growth**: Growth excluding new locations
- **Inventory Turns**: Number of times inventory sold in period
- **Gross Merchandise Value (GMV)**: Total value of merchandise sold
- **Take Rate**: Net Revenue / GMV × 100

### Financial Services
- **Net Interest Margin**: (Interest Income - Interest Expense) / Average Earning Assets
- **Efficiency Ratio**: Non-Interest Expenses / (Net Interest Income + Non-Interest Income)
- **Loan-to-Deposit Ratio**: Total Loans / Total Deposits

### Healthcare
- **Revenue per Bed**: Total Revenue / Number of Beds
- **Occupancy Rate**: Occupied Beds / Total Beds × 100
- **Cost per Patient Day**: Operating Costs / Total Patient Days
